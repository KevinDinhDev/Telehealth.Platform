Telehealth Services

This Agreement applies to Your use of any of the Telehealth Services provided by Telehealth Platform.

a) Telehealth Consultations

  • Toll-free access to telephone or web-based video consultations with licensed physicians.
  • Physician consultations may provide diagnoses, recommend therapy, and potentially provide prescriptions.

b) The Telehealth Programs

Depending on your eligibility, you may have access to a combination of Telehealth Programs described below.

i. General Telehealth Program

Description of the General Telehealth Program and its features.

  • Toll-free access to telephone or web-based video consultations
  • Physician consultations for general medical concerns

ii. Behavioral Health Program

Description of the Behavioral Health Program and its features.

  • Access to licensed mental health professionals
  • Behavioral health content through the Telehealth Platform app

iii. Dermatology Program

Description of the Dermatology Program and its features.

  • Specialized consultations with dermatologists
  • Diagnosis and treatment recommendations for skin-related concerns

c) MyStrength Program / Telehealth for Behavioral Health

Access to the MyStrength mobile application and online platform for tailored behavioral health content.

i. MyStrength Mobile App

Features and services offered through the MyStrength mobile application.

  • Behavioral health content tailored to individual needs
  • Asynchronous interaction with Coaches, Guides, or crisis managers

ii. Licensed Mental Health Professionals

Availability of licensed mental health professionals via Telehealth services.

  • Subscription-based access to mental health professionals
  • Applicable consultation fees for specific services

d) Expert Medical Services

The availability of a toll-free number, web portal, and mobile app to access various expert medical services.

i. Find a Doctor Service

Assistance in identifying physicians in Your geographic region from Telehealth's Global Expert Panel.

  • Matching physicians based on Your specific condition
  • Access to a diverse network of healthcare professionals

ii. Critical Case Support

Expert Medical Opinion for critical cases in an in-patient medical setting.

  • Written report based on provided medical records and information
  • Support for traumatic or catastrophic events such as brain injury, spinal cord injury, etc.

Features and specifications of the Telehealth Services described herein are subject to change at any time without notice.

The physical equipment and supplies provided for the Telehealth Programs shall be collectively, the “Telehealth Equipment & Supplies”.
