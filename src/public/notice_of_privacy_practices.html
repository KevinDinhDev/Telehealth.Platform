Notice Informing Individuals About Nondiscrimination And Accessibility Requirements

Telehealth Platform, which includes Telehealth Platform Health, Inc., Telehealth Platform Health Medical Group P.A., and Telehealth Platform Physicians, P.C., and Telehealth Platform subsidiaries and affiliates, complies with applicable state and Federal civil rights laws. Telehealth Platform does not exclude people, treat them differently, nor discriminate based on gender, sex, age, race, color, national origin, creed, religion, disability, pregnancy, sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, genetic information, marital status, military status, veteran status, or any other characteristic or status protected by state, federal, or local law.

  • Telehealth Platform provides appropriate auxiliary aids and services, which may include qualified sign language interpreters and information in alternate formats, free of charge and in a timely manner, when such aids and services are necessary to ensure an equal opportunity to participate for individuals with disabilities and do not create an undue burden, fundamentally alter the nature of the program or activity, or present a direct threat to the health or safety of others.
  • Telehealth Platform also provides free language services to people whose primary language is not English, such as:
  • Qualified interpreters
  • Information written in other languages
